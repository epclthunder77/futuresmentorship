Mentorship Program Curriculum

  • Week 1: Introduction & Foundations
  • Week 2: Technical Analysis Basics
  • Week 3: Risk Management
  • Week 4: Trading Strategies
  • Week 5: Trading Psychology
  • Week 6: Advanced Analysis
  • Week 7: Live Trading & Mentorship
  • Week 8: Scaling & Long-Term Goals

Week 1: Introduction & Foundations

Establish a solid trading foundation and set expectations.

  • What is trading? (day trading, swing trading, long-term investing)
  • Understanding THE Market (futures, forex, options, etc.)
  • Overview of trading platforms and tools(TradingView, Topstep, OBS, Forex Factory)

Week 2: Technical Analysis Basics

Learn how to analyze price charts and identify patterns.

  • Charting tools and timeframes
  • Key concepts: support, resistance, liquidity, imbalances, big banks
  • Introduction to candlestick patterns

Week 3: Risk Management

Understand how to protect capital and manage trades effectively.

  • Setting risk/reward ratios
  • Position sizing and stop-loss placement
  • Avoiding overtrading and managing emotions

Week 4: Trading Strategies

Learn how to develop and execute trading strategies.

  • Introduction to Fundemental, Sentiment analysis
  • What's my strategy?
  • How to execute properly

Week 5: Trading Psychology

Build mental resilience and develop emotional control for better trading results.

  • Identifying emotional triggers (greed, fear, FOMO)
  • Developing a trading routine and sticking to it
  • Journaling and reviewing trades objectively

Week 6: Advanced Analysis

Dive deeper into market analysis and refine trading skills.

  • Advanced chart patterns (Smart Money Concepts)
  • Combining multiple indicators for confluence
  • Analyzing news and its impact on markets

Week 7: Live Trading & Mentorship

Apply learned skills in real-time with guidance.

  • Live chart analysis with group discussions
  • Identifying opportunities in real-time
  • Reviewing trades immediately after execution

Week 8: Scaling & Long-Term Goals

Prepare for consistent profitability and account growth.

  • Scaling trading positions
  • Building a trading business mindset
  • Setting long-term goals and sticking to a trading plan
